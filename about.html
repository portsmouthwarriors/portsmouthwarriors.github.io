About Portsmouth Warriors
Portsmouth Warriors Cheerleading was started in 2010 and itâ€™s going into its 9th season! We started off with one senior team and have grown to have three Allstar teams, three group stunts and three recreational classes in the 8th season.

We are a not-for-profit club that aims to provide cheerleading to the residents of Portsmouth and the surrounding areas. The club is run by volunteer coaches who do it for the love of the sport, therefore it is a very close knit family orientated club- always putting the athletes first. Our number one aim is to introduce a love of the sport to the athletes, and offer as many opportunities as possible to them.

We have classes for all ages and abilities- we currently have athletes aged 3 to adult, From beginners to higher levels.
